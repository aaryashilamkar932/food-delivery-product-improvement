## 📌 Product Case Study
This project focuses on improving user experience in food delivery applications by solving real-world user problems.
# Food Delivery App Product Improvement

## Problem
Users often face uncertainty about food quality and delivery delays while ordering from food delivery apps.

## User Pain Points
- No real visibility into food preparation
- Confusion about actual delivery time
- Lack of trust in restaurant quality

## Solution
Introduce a feature that provides real-time kitchen status and verified customer food images.

## Proposed Features
1. Live Kitchen Status
Users can see if food is being prepared, packed, or ready.

2. Verified Customer Photos
Customers can upload real images of food after delivery.

3. Smart Delay Prediction
System predicts delays based on traffic, restaurant load, and weather.

## Target Users
- Students
- Working professionals
- Frequent online food order users

## Impact
- Builds trust in platform
- Reduces complaints
- Improves user satisfaction

## Future Scope
- AI-based food quality prediction
- Personalized restaurant recommendations
